Two journeys

This map follows two crossings of the Northwest Passage: the RCMP ship St. Roch, and the Panikpakuttuk family — the Inuit family who made the 1944 crossing and took two years to get home.

  • Play the voyages with the bar at the bottom; the speed slider sets the pace.
  • Tap “Panikpakuttuk family’s journey” for their story — solid = ship, dashed = dog sled.
  • Drag the globe to spin it; the layers button switches basemaps and 3D terrain.

A draft for the Vancouver Maritime Museum. All Inuit content is under the authority of the project’s Inuit consultants and is not cleared for public use.
